GIS and Mapping using QGIS

25, 26 November, 2022 (08)

Course Overview

This course is ideal for participants with a basic knowledge of GIS and who want to learn how to use QGIS to carry out everyday GIS tasks.

The course will enable the participants to work with geodata using QGIS to process data, visualize information, present story-telling maps, and perform geodata analysis.

The course is design for; people in health, resource management, inventory and government or non-governmental organisations.
