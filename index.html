Our Services

Topographic Surveys

Openmaps Surveyors use the best equipment to produce data and drawings that accurately represent details of both natural and built environments. We provide topographic surveys for planning and design for Architectural and Engineering purpose.

  1. - Measure surface elevations of a parcel to generate contour lines that shows the levels across a site

  2. - Measure the size, shape and terrain of a parcel of land

  3. - Locate the existing features like roads, fences, trees, buildings & services lines such as power, fibre optic, sewers & water lines




Setting Out

Openmaps are consulted by contractors or developers to mark out buildings, structures and services based on architectural and engineering drawings.

  1. - Setting out of buildings in a new construction sites

  2. - Road and bridge surveys

  3. - Sewer & drainage networks

  4. - Water supply and sanitation surveys

  5. - Dam construction surveys etc





Property Surveys and Mapping

At Openmaps, we pride ourselves on our ability to consult with our clients to understand requirements to ensure accurate information. Property surveys are as follows:

  1. - Extension of leases

  2. - Locating/mapping property

  3. - Boundary re-establishment or Area determination

  4. - Property subdivision/amalgamation

  5. - Land registration and Sectional surveys





Data Analysis

Openmaps converts our client's data into telling stories that help business to make insightful decisions. We use spatial analytics - the science of where, to bring to clients the visibility and understanding of WHY.

  1. - Data Collection, extraction, exploration & processing

  2. - Data Analytics, visualization, modelling and developing business insights




As-Built Surveys

Using Laser & Lidar scanning. We, Openmaps are able to produce 3D digital models of various objects from 3D digital images for updation of structure designs or masterplan

  1. Models: 3D models of existing buildings which allow architects to place their new design into the existing setting.

  2. As-builts: 3D as built scans of buildings or engineering structures. These scans are used to ensure all aspects of the build are in the correct position.




Drone Surveys

At Openmaps, we employ drones for fast and reliable products for inaccessible or large areas.

  1. Topographic Surveying: For construction, quarrying & mining, civil engineering, landfill and environmental protection projects

  2. Site monitoring and inspection: For volumetric topographic surveys, stockpile and spoil heap calculations, earthworks material excavation calculations, landfill, quarrying and mining void calculations

  3. Mapping: For site infrastructure and facilities management, highways mapping for construction and managements
